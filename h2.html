About Us

Home Away From Home Pet Spa understands that your pets are part of your family and we want to care for them like you would. Your pet is unique—this means individual care and comfort, feeding, bedding, and grooming need to be as individualized as your fur babies.

With years of experience, great reviews, and a reputation that is second to none in the pet care industry, people travel from Northern California and Southern Oregon to entrust us with their fur babies.

We are an enthusiastic, motivated team of positive pet professionals dedicated to excellence and outstanding customer service.

Over 20 Years of Loving Pets

Sandra and Jim opened Home Away From Home in 1998 with a goal to take the very best care of all the lovely pets that come to them. The staff truly cares for each and every pet, and the facility is always kept in premium condition—making it the top pet spa in Southern Oregon.

Our Services

Dog Boarding & Daycare

Private rooms for each dog or family, personalized report cards, group playtime in our fenced yard with jungle gym, pool, and toys. Extra TLC, one-on-one time, or potty breaks available. Vaccinations required: Rabies, DHP (Distemper/Parvo), Bordetella (at least 5 days prior).

Cat Boarding & Daycare

Quiet, self-contained area with Kitty Condos, Playrooms, or Window Suites. Multiple levels, daily recess/play time, specialized care (medications, nail trims). Included: litter, bedding, toys, treats. Vaccinations required: RCP, Feline Leukemia, Rabies.

Grooming & Bathing

Professional grooming, bathing, and styling for dogs and cats. We provide individualized care tailored to your pet's needs. Contact us for details and appointments.

Training & Agility

Obedience training, agility courses, and specialized sessions. Our experienced team helps your dog learn and have fun. Contact us for class details and scheduling.
